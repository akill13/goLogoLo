Help

The goLogoLo is a desktop Java application for creating and editing logos.

Step 1: Tap a shape

Then choose a size.

Step 2: Text

Click on the Add Text Icon button. Write whatever you want and then tap ok

Step 3: Add color

Add whatever color you like.

Step 4: Save the Logo

Once you've completed your work, you can press the save button and give your file a name.

Step 5: Export your Logos

Press the Export button when you wish to export your logo a Web page summarizing the list in full.
